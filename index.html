Jared Taylor

About Mr. Taylor

Education

1973 — BA, Philosophy, Yale University

Professional Experience

  • 1974 — 1975: News Editor, Washington Times (now defunct)
  • 1978 — 1981: International Lending Officer, Manufacturers Hanover Trust Co. (New York and Tokyo)
  • 1983 — 1988: Contributing Editor and West Coast Editor, PC Magazine
  • 1982 — Present: Consultant to American companies doing business in Japan
  • 1991 — Present: Editor, American Renaissance
  • 1994 — Present: President, New Century Foundation

Books

  • Shadows of the Rising Sun: A Critical View of the Japanese Miracle, by Jared Taylor (WilliamMorrow & Co., 1983, 336 pp.)
  • The Tyranny of the New and Other Essays, by Jared Taylor (Kinseido Publishing, Tokyo, 1992, 89 pp.)
  • Paved With Good Intentions: The Failure of Race Relations in Contemporary America, by Jared Taylor (Carroll & Graf, 1992, 416 pp.)
  • The Real American Dilemma: Race, Immigration, and the Future of America, by Jared Taylor (New Century Books, 1998, 139 pp.)
  • A Race Against Time: Racial Heresies for the 21st Century, by Jared Taylor (New Century Books, 2003, 331 pp.)
  • White Identity: Racial Consciousness for the 21st Century, by Jared Taylor (New Century Books, 2011, 340 pp.)

Personal Background

Jared Taylor was born in Japan, and has taught Japanese at Harvard Summer School. Mr. Taylor has also done course work for advanced degree entirely in French. Also, he is an accomplished classical and jazz musician.
